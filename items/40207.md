## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still installs macOS LaunchAgent stdout/stderr under `$OPENCLAW_STATE_DIR/logs` (default `~/.openclaw/logs`) and writes those paths directly into the LaunchAgent plist, so the reported symlinked-state-dir failure mode is still plausible and there is no evidence on `main` of the proposed fix to move launchd logs to `~/Library/Logs` or resolve symlink targets before writing the plist.
