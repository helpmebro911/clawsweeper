## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still gives every native `sessions_spawn` call a fresh `agent:<id>:subagent:<uuid>` session key, and the public tool/config/docs surface does not provide a general warm-reuse or pooled native subagent path. The only persistence documented today is thread-bound `mode:"session"` routing on supported channels, which is narrower than the issue report.

## Evidence
