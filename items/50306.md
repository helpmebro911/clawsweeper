## Summary

Keep open. This is a recent, concrete regression report for the bundled LINE channel, with follow-up details that the credentials remain in config and that two LINE webhook paths are served through ngrok. Current main has LINE webhook route registration and custom-path support, but I did not find source-backed evidence that the specific daily route loss/404 behavior is fixed or obsolete.

## Evidence

- **Issue context describes a concrete current failure pattern:** The reporter says LINE Developer Verify succeeds, then after a day LINE messages stop and Verify returns 404; editing/saving/reloading the gateway restores the same config. They later clarified that the webhooks use an ngrok tunnel and two webhook paths for two channels.

This is relevant, but it does not prove the long-running route cannot disappear after idle time or a daily cycle.
