## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement this PR’s behavior: `message:received` and `message:sent` internal hooks are still fired as side effects with no delivery path for `event.messages`, and the loop-suppression plumbing proposed in the PR is not present. The report remains valid rather than obsolete.
