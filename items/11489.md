## Summary

Keep #11489 open. Current main still has DM/node pairing only; group access remains open/disabled/allowlist with no `groupPolicy: "pairing"`, no pending group approval state, no group approve/reject CLI, no owner-visible group-join notification path, and no pending read-only group-agent mode. Related #8495 and PR #42400 cover narrower group hygiene or passive-reply suppression ideas, not the core group pairing flow requested here.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #11489 open for maintainer/product design. The likely implementation is an additive core/channel contract: add `groupPolicy: "pairing"` to config/schema/docs, persist pending group approvals keyed by channel/account/group ID, add CLI approve/reject semantics, emit owner-visible group-join or first-seen-unconfigured-group notifications, teach group-capable channels such as Telegram and WhatsApp to honor that state, and decide whether pending read-only observation belongs in the same change or a narrower follow-up coordinated with #8495 and PR #42400.
