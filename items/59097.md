## Summary

Keep open. Current main still distinguishes buffered vs non-buffered reply dispatch, but the common external-channel helpers and examples continue to steer plugin authors toward the buffered path that intentionally chunks long buffered output. That leaves the reported SSE/non-chunked-channel breakage plausible and not obviously resolved on `main`.
