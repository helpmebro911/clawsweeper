## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still does not thread `requesterSenderId` or `senderIsOwner` into `ChannelAgentToolFactory`, even though adjacent plugin-facing discovery/tool paths already carry that trusted sender context. This PR is therefore not implemented on `main`, and the linked DMWork use case remains concrete rather than stale or incoherent.
