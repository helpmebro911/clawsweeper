## Summary

Keep open. Current main still does not implement broad gateway-start auto-application of doctor fixes or a `doctor.autoFix` option. It has narrow startup recovery/maintenance paths, while the main doctor repair flow remains explicit, and the issue discussion includes recent safety evidence that automatic `doctor --fix` on startup can silently rewrite intentional config.
