## Summary

Current main still lacks the requested configurable Telegram processing-indicator message lifecycle. Telegram has adjacent preview, tool-progress, typing, and reaction features, but none send an immediate standalone placeholder such as `⌛️` on message receipt and then delete or edit that placeholder on reply/failure. The related PRs #43021 and #43720 were closed unmerged, so they are prior art rather than a superseding implementation.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep tracking this issue until the Telegram plugin implements an opt-in processing-indicator lifecycle, likely by adding Telegram config/schema/docs/tests and wiring `extensions/telegram/src/bot-message-dispatch.ts` to send a visible placeholder immediately after receiving an eligible message, delete it after a successful final reply, and edit or replace it on dispatch failure. PRs #43021 and #43720 are useful prior art but need a fresh implementation against current main.
