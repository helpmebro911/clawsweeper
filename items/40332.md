## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement per-binding or per-agent ACPX `permissionMode`/`nonInteractivePermissions` overrides. Those permissions are still configured only at the global `plugins.entries.acpx.config` layer, while agent ACP defaults and ACP binding overrides only carry `agent/backend/mode/cwd` plus binding `label`.

## Evidence
