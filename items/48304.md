## Summary

Keep open. Current main has partial sender-aware plumbing and coarse tool filters, but it does not implement the requested gateway-level, per-action/per-sender authorization for `message.send` or a generic pre-tool authorization policy. The related guardrail PRs in the provided context were closed unmerged, and their proposed `GuardrailProvider` surface is not present on current main.

## Evidence
