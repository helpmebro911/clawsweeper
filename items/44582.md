## Summary

Current `main` already covers the report's requested hardening: context growth is configurable and enforced via `contextTokens`, Discord listener/inbound timeouts are exposed as supported config, nested work is scoped per target session to reduce unrelated lane blocking, and typing cleanup has explicit post-run safety nets. The issue reads as a rollup of behavior that has since been implemented and documented on `main`.
