## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` fixes several of the original examples, including Slack before Synology Chat in the channel index and Venice before Vercel AI Gateway in the provider index, and the provider nav now has the originally cited Claude/Cloudflare, GLM/Google, Mistral/Moonshot, and OpenCode/OpenCode Go orderings corrected. However, the broader docs-ordering issue is still valid: `docs/providers/index.md` currently places Gradium before GLM models and Google, and `docs/docs.json` still has visibly non-alphabetical channel nav groups without an explicit runtime/detection-order exception. The closed related PRs were not merged and there is no narrower open canonical follow-up in the provided context.
