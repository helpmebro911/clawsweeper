## Summary

Keep open. Current main still does not implement the requested native subagent session reuse for expert agents, and Teams still has no subagent thread-binding hook path. ACP has an explicit resumeSessionId path, but that is limited to runtime="acp" and does not solve native runtime="subagent" Teams spawns.
