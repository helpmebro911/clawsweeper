## Summary

Keep #49736 open. Current main has partial custom-provider auth improvements, including env SecretRef resolution and synthetic loopback auth, but the reported `custom-192-168-0-222-11434`/`ollama-local` failure mode is still plausible in source: `ollama-local` is a known non-secret marker, and custom-provider auth rejects non-secret markers as usable configured keys unless they are known env-var markers. The related PR #49805 was closed unmerged and addressed SecretRef handling, not the reporter’s later hyphen/`ollama-local` workaround.

## Evidence

- **Issue discussion identifies key-string workaround:** The reporter commented that changing the configured apiKey from `ollama-local` to `ollamalocal` made OpenClaw work again. That makes the non-secret marker path relevant to the reported failure, even though another commenter hypothesized provider identity drift and PR #49805 later targeted SecretRef handling.
