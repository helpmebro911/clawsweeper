## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still appears to reproduce the reported failure path: Slack scope inspection only calls `auth.scopes` and `apps.permissions.info`, collects any returned errors, and then renders that error text directly in `openclaw channels capabilities`. I did not find an alternate fallback on `main` that would avoid `unknown_method` for this command.
