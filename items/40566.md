## Summary

Keep open. Current main still has the reported startup race: gateway startup kicks off ACP identity reconciliation immediately after plugin services start, while the bundled ACPX service only marks the backend ready later from a detached async probe. That means `ACP_BACKEND_UNAVAILABLE` remains a plausible startup outcome, and I did not find a current-main fix or regression test covering the requested wait/suppress behavior.

## Evidence

- **Gateway startup still reconciles immediately:** `startGatewaySidecars()` awaits `startPluginServices(...)` and then immediately calls `getAcpSessionManager().reconcilePendingSessionIdentities(...)` under `if (params.cfg.acp?.enabled)`, with no readiness wait or unavailable-error suppression.
  - file: [src/gateway/server-startup-post-attach.ts:299](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/d4e93e791bc5093ea14666bf4d8202b45a5132b4/src/gateway/server-startup-post-attach.ts#L299)
