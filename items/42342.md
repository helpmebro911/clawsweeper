## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` already includes the cron payload-kind idempotence fix in a different path, but the PR's systemd inferred-user-bus retry is still not implemented on `main`, and the submitted systemd change had a review-blocking null-dereference called out in review. That is partial supersession, not a safe auto-close reason for this PR.
