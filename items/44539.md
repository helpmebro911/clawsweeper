## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still has a plausible false-positive path for this exact Telegram multi-account shape: doctor goes through the health summary for a single preferred/default account, while Telegram marks later same-token accounts as `configured=false`, and `status` separately reports the channel as configured if any account is configured.
