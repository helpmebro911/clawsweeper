## Summary

Keep #39406 open. Current main still lacks the requested normal-chat delivery policy for transient tool-error warnings: no `toolErrorDelivery` or global mutating-warning suppression surface exists, mutating tool errors still force warning payloads even after assistant text, and the only broad suppression hook remains a per-run/heartbeat option. Current main has a useful same-action retry cleanup, but that is only a partial mitigation rather than the requested configurable `all` / `final` / `off` behavior.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #39406 open for a documented normal-chat tool-error delivery policy, likely near `resolveToolErrorWarningPolicy` and `buildEmbeddedRunPayloads`, with config type/schema/help/docs updates and focused payload tests. Preserve the current safe default, keep the same-action retry cleanup, and coordinate with #39631 and #51091 without treating either unmerged related item as shipped coverage.
