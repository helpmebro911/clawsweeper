## Summary

Keep open. Current main has partial hardening around allow-always persistence, interpreter inline eval, and Windows-only argPattern matching, but the central request is not implemented: cross-platform binary+args allowlist enforcement and progressive approval-log pattern inference remain missing. The issue also has supportive discussion from other users and no protected maintainer signal requiring closure.
