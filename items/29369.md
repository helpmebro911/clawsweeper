## Summary

Keep #29369 open. Current main still does not expose or honor a spawn-time `tools` allowlist on `sessions_spawn`; tool policy remains configured through global/provider/agent/group/sandbox/subagent config rather than per call. The prior ClawSweeper keep-open comment remains accurate against current main, and the provided related items do not supersede this specific request.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #29369 open until maintainers design and implement spawn-time tool policy for `sessions_spawn`. The likely implementation needs schema/type additions, executor parsing, propagation through both native and ACP spawn paths, integration with effective tool-policy resolution, docs, and tests covering precedence against `tools.allow`, provider policy, `agents.list[].tools`, group policy, sandbox policy, `tools.subagents.tools`, and deny-wins behavior.
