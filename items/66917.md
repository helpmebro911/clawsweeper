## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` already exposes several of the requested mitigations and diagnostics for WhatsApp, but I did not find high-confidence evidence that the reported persistent 408/499 disconnect pattern, dual-account auth corruption, or long-run group inbound failure has been fixed or become obsolete on `main`. This report is recent, plausible, and still actionable.

## Evidence

- **Issue reports unresolved runtime failures, not just a feature request:** The issue body describes recurring 408/499 disconnects, suspected auth-state corruption with two active WhatsApp accounts, and a later silent group inbound failure that only a full gateway restart clears. There are no follow-up comments or maintainer timeline items narrowing it to an obsolete configuration mistake.
