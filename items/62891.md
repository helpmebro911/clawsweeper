## Summary

Keep this issue open. The discussion narrows it to a cron/channel readiness race during startup and SIGUSR1/config hot reload, and current main still starts cron after channel startup handoff rather than after channel transport readiness. Startup catch-up and automatic timer execution can still run while Discord-style channels are in startup grace or disconnected.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this issue open and implement a bounded cron readiness gate at the gateway/cron boundary. The likely path is to add a delivery-aware wait around activateGatewayScheduledServices and hot cron reload so startup catch-up and automatic timer execution for channel-delivered cron jobs wait for relevant configured channels to become connected/healthy, a configurable timeout to expire, or readiness to fail with clear diagnostics. Add focused coverage for full startup, config.patch/SIGUSR1 hot reload, Discord-style asynchronous readiness, missed-job catch-up, and no-channel or webhook-only cron jobs.
