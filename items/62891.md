## Summary

Keep this issue open. The reporter’s comments narrow the bug from session-store reconciliation to a cron/channel readiness race, and current main still permits cron startup catch-up or hot-reload cron jobs to run before Discord or other async channels have reached connected readiness.

## Best Possible Solution

Implement a bounded cron startup and reload readiness gate at the gateway/cron boundary. Delay automatic timer execution and startup catch-up for channel-delivered cron work until the relevant configured delivery channels are connected/healthy, a configurable wait expires, or readiness fails with clear diagnostics. Cover full startup, config.patch/SIGUSR1 hot reload, Discord-style async readiness, and existing /readyz/channel-health behavior with targeted tests.

## Risks / Open Questions

- The reporter's exact multi-minute Discord timing is environment-dependent, but current source still permits the ordering race.
- A fix needs bounded, delivery-aware semantics so webhook-only jobs, no-channel jobs, and optional or unhealthy channels do not block cron indefinitely.
- The existing /readyz startup grace may not be suitable as-is for cron gating; cron likely needs an explicit wait policy tied to configured delivery targets.
