## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement this PR’s requested behavior: `llm_input`, `llm_output`, and `after_tool_call` are still audit-only `void` hooks, the embedded runner does not await/apply hook mutations or blocking, and the tool adapter still bypasses `after_tool_call` mutation handling.

## Evidence

  - file: [src/plugins/hooks.ts:595](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/a958b6e72328d1d922a6d133b1a70e866faf4013/src/plugins/hooks.ts#L595)
