## Summary

Keep #20786 open. Current main still does not implement Telegram Business message handling: business update names exist in the allowed-update constants, but there are no business_message/business_connection handlers, no business payloads in dedupe or sequential lane selection, no business_connection_id mapping for outbound replies, and no docs/tests for the feature. Related PR #53629 is still open and unmerged, so this issue remains the canonical tracker.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #20786 open as the canonical Telegram Business support tracker. Land PR #53629 or an equivalent Telegram plugin implementation that registers business_connection and business_message handlers before the normal message handler, routes business messages through the existing inbound pipeline, extends dedupe and sequential keys, persists/maps business_connection_id for outbound replies, and adds focused tests plus user-facing Telegram docs.
