## Summary

Keep #20786 open. Current main still only exposes Telegram Business update names in the allowed-update list; it does not register business_message/business_connection handlers, include business payloads in dedupe or sequential lanes, persist/map business_connection_id for outbound replies, or document/test Telegram Business support. Related PR #53629 is still an unmerged implementation attempt, so the issue remains the canonical tracker.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #20786 open as the canonical Telegram Business support tracker. Land PR #53629 or an equivalent extensions/telegram implementation that registers business_connection and business_message handlers, routes business messages through the existing inbound pipeline, extends dedupe and sequential keys, persists/maps business_connection_id for outbound replies, and adds focused tests plus user-facing Telegram docs.
