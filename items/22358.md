## Summary

Keep open. Current main has subagent lifecycle plugin hooks, including `subagent_ended`, but they do not implement the requested post-completion/pre-announcement extension point: the completion hook is observation-only, lacks announcement/result/usage mutation fields, and for completion-mode runs is explicitly deferred until after announce delivery resolves or gives up. Related issues overlap, especially #8354 and #66638, but neither clearly supersedes this narrower request for a programmable hook that can inspect completion output and enrich/suppress/fallback the completion announcement.
