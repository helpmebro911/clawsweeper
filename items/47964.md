## Summary

Keep open. Current main writes new OpenAI Codex OAuth login results in the normalized `access` / `refresh` / `expires` shape, but the auth-profile loader does not normalize likely legacy OAuth aliases such as `accessToken`, `refreshToken`, or `expiresAt`. Runtime eligibility still drops OAuth profiles when both normalized `access` and `refresh` are missing, so the specific compatibility/migration question in #47964 remains actionable. The related closed ClawSweeper items cover profile-id drift and provider injection, not this stored-field-shape gap.
