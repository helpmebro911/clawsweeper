## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not show a core fix for the reported Claude subscription billing behavior; instead, it documents a community proxy workaround and still explicitly notes that Gateway Claude subscription runs can hit extra-usage billing for third-party app usage. The issue discussion's reported workaround is therefore acknowledged on `main`, but the underlying OpenClaw-core concern is not clearly resolved.
