## Summary

Keep this PR open. Current main still lacks the central postHookActions event mechanism, session-memory block/content controls, filename uniqueness hardening, and slug-generation tool hardening; the related open PRs/issues are dependent or complementary, not replacements.

Treat #38162 as dependent redirect-path work and #64181 as complementary slug-response validation, not replacements.
