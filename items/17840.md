## Summary

Keep open. Current main still treats inbound reactions as passive notification/system-event context, and I found no implemented opt-in such as `reactionTriggersTurn`, `awaitReaction`, or a channel config that wakes an agent turn on reactions. The issue discussion has multiple concrete Telegram, Slack, and Discord workflows, and the requested own-scope opt-in remains meaningful.
