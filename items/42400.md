## Summary

Current `main` does not implement the proposed `neverReply` config or an equivalent shipped replacement. The docs and config surface still describe group handling as `groupPolicy` plus mention gating only, and the plugin hook seam that might support a ClawHub/plugin alternative is still limited to plugin-bound conversations rather than general channel traffic. This PR is not obsolete, not incoherent, and not safely closable under the allowed reasons.
