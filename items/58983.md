## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement Feishu native exec-approval cards/buttons for pending approvals; Feishu only has same-chat `/approve` authorization today, while its existing card UX is limited to other quick actions such as `/new` and `/reset`.

## Evidence

- **Feishu approval capability is auth-only:** The Feishu channel registers `approvalCapability: feishuApprovalAuth`, with no native/render/delivery wiring at the channel entrypoint.
  - file: [extensions/feishu/src/channel.ts:666](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/11cffb230013c2d8bd1e0e18da04b0b985b8abd5/extensions/feishu/src/channel.ts#L666)

- **Feishu approval capability resolves approvers only:** `feishuApprovalAuth` is built with `createResolvedApproverActionAuthAdapter(...)` and only resolves approvers from `allowFrom`; there is no Feishu-native approval delivery or button rendering here.
  - file: [extensions/feishu/src/approval-auth.ts:15](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/11cffb230013c2d8bd1e0e18da04b0b985b8abd5/extensions/feishu/src/approval-auth.ts#L15)
