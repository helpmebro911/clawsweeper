## Summary

Do not auto-close #50998. Current main still does not implement the requested iOS Contacts/Calendar permission restoration: the app bundle lacks Contacts/Calendar usage-description keys, Contacts `.notDetermined` still fast-fails, and Calendar uses the shared no-prompt authorization helper. The related #51011 is complementary settings UX and remains open; it does not supersede this service-layer bug-fix PR.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #50998 open for maintainer review. The best path is to land or revise a focused iOS permission fix that adds the required plist keys, handles Calendar first-use and write-only upgrade with cancellation-safe prompts, and resolves Contacts permission prompting through an explicitly interactive path, likely coordinated with #51011 rather than silently prompting from headless `node.invoke`.
