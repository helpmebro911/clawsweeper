## Summary

Keep open. Current main has lifecycle, item, tool, and chat-abort events, but it does not implement the requested atomic intent→execution→commit/abort contract or a mandatory machine-readable abort blocker for announced pre-action intent. The issue is not protected, has no comments, and is broad, but the requested core reliability capability remains meaningfully missing rather than clearly implemented, duplicate, ClawHub-scoped, or incoherent.
