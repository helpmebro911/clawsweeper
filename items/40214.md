## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement ZAI as a web-search provider, and the PR targets an older core-owned web-search path that has since moved to plugin-owned provider registration. There is no strong basis for `implemented_on_main`, `cannot_reproduce`, `clawhub`, or `incoherent` closure.

## Evidence
