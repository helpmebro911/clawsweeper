## Summary

Keep open. The reported OpenRouter + Mistral strict9 tool-call ID gap still appears present on current main: OpenRouter owns a passthrough Gemini replay policy, but that policy does not enable `sanitizeToolCallIds` or `toolCallIdMode: "strict9"` for Mistral-family OpenRouter model ids. The reporter’s linked PRs/comments are relevant fix attempts, but the current source does not show the requested behavior implemented.
