## Summary

Keep #49086 open. Current main still ships the AGENTS.md workspace template with the reported `memory/heartbeat-state.json` raw Unix timestamp example and no instruction to obtain timestamps via `date +%s` or another tool call. Newer `HEARTBEAT.md` task-mode support is relevant and reduces the need for model-written state, but it does not update or remove the unsafe template guidance that the issue reports.
