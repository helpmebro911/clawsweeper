## Summary

Current `main` does not implement the requested load-time behavior of appending `<workspace>/skills/<skill-name>/NOTES.md` into a bundled skill’s `SKILL.md`. The existing product surfaces are different: whole-skill precedence overrides, always-injected `TOOLS.md` for local notes, and the optional Skill Workshop plugin for writing durable workspace skills. Since the issue is still coherent and those alternatives do not clearly make the request obsolete or clearly out of core scope, the conservative cleanup call is to keep it open.
