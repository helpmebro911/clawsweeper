## Summary

Keep #37131 open. Current main has partial direct/no-LLM paths, including `POST /tools/invoke`, plugin slash commands, and skill commands that can dispatch directly to a declared tool, but the umbrella request is not implemented across the requested coding-tool, cron, CLI, and generic slash-command surfaces. Related #63919 and #18160 are useful open slices, but neither supersedes the whole request.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #37131 open as the umbrella product/API request until maintainers either ship a single security-reviewed direct invocation contract across HTTP, cron, CLI, and slash-command surfaces, or split and link canonical narrower trackers for all remaining work. Related open items #63919 (HTTP coding tools) and #18160 (cron direct exec) should remain referenced as active slices, but they do not cover the whole request.
