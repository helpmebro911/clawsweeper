## Summary

Keep open. Current main still appears to preserve Codex app-server `inputTokens` as `input` while also preserving `cachedInputTokens` as `cacheRead`, and the shared session/status accounting still treats those fields as additive. The recent maintainer comment also reports fresh 2026.4.24-era user signal and explicitly says this does not look fixed on current main.

## Evidence

- **Codex projector still maps both fields additively:** `normalizeCodexTokenUsage` maps `inputTokens`/`input_tokens` to `input` and `cachedInputTokens`/cache aliases to `cacheRead`, then passes that shape to `normalizeUsage`; there is no Codex-specific subtraction at the source.
  - file: [extensions/codex/src/app-server/event-projector.ts:908](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/81a41fe5bee350c6a035bfcc458eb8be380afe2c/extensions/codex/src/app-server/event-projector.ts#L908)
