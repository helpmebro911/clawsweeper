## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still contains the reported unguarded Feishu `.trim()` path, and the parser chain can still propagate runtime-undefined message content into that call. Prior issue discussion referenced candidate fixes, but the checked-out `main` SHA `cc0f3e0e40364023cc3a39227a411898ee68ce3b` does not show the guard on the affected path.

## Evidence

  - file: [extensions/feishu/src/monitor.message-handler.ts:210](https://github.com/openclaw/openclaw/blob/cc0f3e0e40364023cc3a39227a411898ee68ce3b/extensions/feishu/src/monitor.message-handler.ts#L210)
