## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still defaults ACPX to non-interactive permission behavior that the report and follow-up comments identified as a hang/fail trigger, and the docs still describe ACP sessions failing early with little output or stalling indefinitely. I did not find evidence on `main` that OpenClaw now applies the reported workaround automatically or otherwise closes out this class of ACP spawn hangs.
