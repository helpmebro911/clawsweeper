## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still auto-delivers visible WhatsApp replies through `deliverWebReply`, and that path always sends audio with `ptt: true` and no per-reply or config switch to suppress auto-delivery. The WhatsApp config surface does not expose a `deliverWebReply` disable/toggle, and the documented silent-reply path is not a reliable temporary workaround for direct chats because bare `NO_REPLY` is rewritten by default there.

## Evidence
