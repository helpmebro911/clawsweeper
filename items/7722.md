## Summary

#7722 should stay open. Current main still implements only the narrower `tools.fs.workspaceOnly` boolean guard; it does not accept the requested `tools.fileAccess`/`mode`/`allowedPaths`/`denyPaths` config surface, and exec still has no filesystem allow/deny policy enforcement.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #7722 open until OpenClaw implements a versioned filesystem access policy surface, likely under `tools.fs` or a successor config contract, with deny-wins `allowedPaths`/`denyPaths` rules enforced across read/write/edit/apply_patch/local media and a defensible exec strategy, or until maintainers explicitly narrow/reject that product direction. Treat `tools.fs.workspaceOnly`, #5948, #8719, and PR #60981 as related partial/contextual work rather than completion.
