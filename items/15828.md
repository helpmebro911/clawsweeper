## Summary

Keep #15828 open. Current main still implements bundled session-memory as a command-only hook for /new and /reset. Automatic daily/idle rollovers archive transcripts and emit typed session lifecycle hooks, but they do not invoke the bundled session-memory hook; shutdown, periodic/message-count checkpointing, and LLM summary output also remain missing.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #15828 open unless maintainers intentionally split it. The best product path is to extend bundled session-memory with configurable idle, shutdown, command, and message-count/periodic triggers; define dedupe and summary-generation policy; wire the chosen lifecycle path through source, config schema/docs, and focused tests; or explicitly split daily/idle rollover work into the related PRs while leaving shutdown/checkpoint/summary behavior tracked here.
