## Summary

Keep #15828 open. Current main still implements bundled `session-memory` as a `/new` and `/reset` command hook. Daily/idle rollovers emit typed plugin session lifecycle hooks, but they do not dispatch the bundled internal hook. Shutdown-triggered saves, message-count/periodic checkpoints, configurable trigger options, and LLM-generated summary output also remain missing. Related open PRs #61675 and #71792 may cover the daily/idle subset later, but they do not supersede the broader issue yet.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #15828 open unless maintainers intentionally split it. The concrete path is to extend bundled `session-memory` with explicit configurable idle, shutdown, command, and message-count/periodic triggers; define dedupe and summary-generation behavior; wire the selected lifecycle path through source, config schema/docs, and focused tests; and treat #61675/#71792 as possible narrower daily/idle implementations rather than full replacements for this broader request.
