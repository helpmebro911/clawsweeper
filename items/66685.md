## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still warns on every exec approval followup dispatch error, so the PR's suppression for `unknown or expired approval id` is not already implemented, and there is no strong close reason. The review discussion also indicates the PR's red CI was caused by unrelated base/parity failures rather than this 2-file change.
