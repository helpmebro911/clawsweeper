## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still strips a trailing own-line `NO_REPLY` and delivers the preceding text, so the PR addresses a real unresolved bug. The timeline’s cross-reference to #46626 also suggests this patch may need follow-up coverage for other delivery paths rather than being closed as obsolete.
