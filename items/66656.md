## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still derives `lastMessagePreview` from the last trailing user/assistant transcript entry without excluding `diagnostic.heartbeat`, and that field is still surfaced through session-list conversation descriptors. The PR remains materially relevant, and the provided review discussion describes it as a small, safe fix with a matching regression test.
