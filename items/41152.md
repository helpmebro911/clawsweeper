## Summary

Keep open. Current main still builds the async gateway approval follow-up from the last 400 characters of aggregated output and passes it through whitespace-normalizing formatting, which matches the reported truncation and newline-stripping behavior. The linked fix PR #41170 was closed unmerged, and I found no current-main replacement in the inspected path.
