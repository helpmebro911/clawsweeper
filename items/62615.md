## Summary

Keep open. Current main has several bounded retry and recovery mechanisms, but I did not find the requested gateway/session-level circuit breaker that tracks consecutive failures for a session, marks the session unhealthy, exposes operator/UI state, and blocks or rotates future automatic retries after a configurable threshold.

## Evidence

- **Issue scope:** #62615 asks for a gateway/runtime session-level circuit breaker that trips after repeated session failures, stops automatic retries, marks the session unhealthy, emits visible alerts, and optionally starts a fresh session. The related #62614 documents the retry-storm/cost symptom, but it is closed and does not supersede this feature request.
