Keep #71602 open. Keep the issue open and ask for one complete restart cycle of `docker compose logs openclaw-gateway`, `docker inspect` state including `ExitCode` and `OOMKilled`, the exact image tag or digest, redacted `.env`, whether `OPENCLAW_DISABLE_BONJOUR=1`, and the newest `openclaw gateway stability --bundle latest` output if present.
