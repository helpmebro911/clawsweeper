## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still surfaces the reported WhatsApp login timeout as a generic failure, the only explicit recovery paths are for 515 restart-after-pairing and 401 logged-out cases, and the latest WhatsApp login work on `main` is about QR sync rather than this 408/opening-handshake regression. The issue is also recent and corroborated by a second Windows 11 report.
