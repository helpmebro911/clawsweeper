## Summary

Keep open. Current main has partial related surfaces, but it still does not provide an admin command to view/manage approved WhatsApp senders with DM policy, group policy, and last active. `pairing list` is pending-only, and `directory peers` is ID/name lookup rather than the requested permissions/contact management view.
