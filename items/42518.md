## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still does not load `~/.openclaw/secrets/.env`, and enabled Telegram SecretRefs that do not resolve still fail secrets preflight/startup instead of degrading that one bot account. Only inactive Telegram surfaces downgrade to warnings.

## Evidence
