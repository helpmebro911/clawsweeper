## Summary

Keep open. The current main tree has the earlier Moonshot tool-call ID fix and an optional `thinking.keep=all` setting, but I did not find a current Moonshot replay/compaction fix that restores or synthesizes `reasoning_content` for assistant tool-call messages after LCM/context-engine assembly or cross-model fallback. The issue is fresh, has concrete reproduction details, and remains actionable in OpenClaw.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #71491 open. The best product/code path is a provider-owned Moonshot replay fix, likely in the Moonshot thinking stream/replay hooks, plus regression coverage for Kimi K2.6 with thinking enabled after context-engine/LCM assembly and cross-model fallback. The fix should either preserve `thinkingSignature: "reasoning_content"` through the OpenClaw boundary or synthesize the Moonshot-required blank/top-level `reasoning_content` for assistant tool-call turns when replaying to Kimi, without weakening other OpenAI-compatible providers.

## Evidence

## Risks / Open Questions

- Closing this now would likely discard a concrete, recent regression that is not covered by current Moonshot tests.
- A fix needs care because `reasoning_content` preservation is provider-specific: Kimi/Moonshot, DeepSeek, Z.AI, and OpenAI Responses do not all share the same replay contract.
- The LCM-side lossless-claw issue may also need a fix, but OpenClaw still has an actionable boundary to repair or validate provider replay after context-engine assembly and cross-model fallback.
