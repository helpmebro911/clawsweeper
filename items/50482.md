## Summary

Keep open. Current main has internal inbound-audio detection for reply/TTS handling and Telegram voice notes are transcribed into an audio-framed BodyForAgent, but the requested agent-visible voice/message-type flag is still not present in inbound metadata or MsgContext. Telegram also collapses voice and audio into the same audio placeholder/media MIME path, so agents still cannot reliably distinguish a Telegram voice note from other audio or text via first-class context metadata.
