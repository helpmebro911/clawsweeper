## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still resolves Telegram inbound voice/audio context, including preflight transcription for audio-only messages, before the reply pipeline installs the typing callback, so `typingMode: "instant"` still starts at reply/model-run time rather than immediately on voice receipt.
