## Summary

Keep #70440 open. Current main has several related empty-turn fixes, including suppression after committed messaging delivery and a group-only silent-empty path, but it still does not provide the requested global/per-channel option to silently swallow genuine empty-turn fallback delivery for direct or Signal/TTS contexts. The maintainer discussion explicitly says this remains valid after the #70396/#70623 fallback fix.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep the issue open and implement an explicit empty/incomplete-turn delivery policy in shared config, preserving the current error default while allowing direct or per-surface/per-channel TTS deployments to suppress final fallback delivery and log the failure server-side.
