## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still allows other Discord bots through when `allowBots=true` (or when `allowBots="mentions"` and they mention the bot), and the repo has no bot-pair loop protection, cooldown, or suppression config matching the report. Existing protections only cover self-messages and some webhook-echo cases, not two distinct bots mentioning each other.
