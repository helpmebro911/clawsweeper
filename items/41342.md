## Summary

Keep open. Current main has an explicit `responseFormat` escape hatch, but the reported Telegram + OpenAI-compatible TTS path still defaults to Opus for voice-note targets, and the linked Kokoro discussion points at Opus-specific truncation. That is not strong enough to close as implemented, cannot-reproduce, duplicate, or ClawHub scope.
