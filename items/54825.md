## Summary

Keep this issue open. Current main has adjacent protections for raw provider/runtime text and Telegram delivery-error throttling, but it still intentionally surfaces billing/rate-limit provider failures as user-visible channel replies and does not provide the requested WhatsApp/Telegram suppress, custom-message, or owner-only policy for generated provider-error payloads.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep this open and implement a shared outbound error-reply policy before channel delivery for provider/runtime error payloads. The policy should support at least normal reply, silent, custom message, and owner-only routing, with per-channel/account/group/topic overrides for WhatsApp and Telegram, preserved detailed diagnostics in logs/control UI, and focused tests for WhatsApp group billing suppression/custom text, Telegram group suppression, owner DM visibility, and unchanged internal diagnostics.
