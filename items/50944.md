## Summary

Current main has implemented the WhatsApp quote-reply half of #50944, but the outbound @mention half is still missing. The WhatsApp outbound path builds text/media payloads and quote options, but does not derive @+number mentions or attach Baileys contextInfo.mentionedJid. A related open item (#39879) tracks outbound mentions specifically, but because #50944 itself reports a still-missing bundled WhatsApp behavior, this is not a conservative auto-close candidate.
