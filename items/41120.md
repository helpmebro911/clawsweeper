## Summary

Keep #41120 open. Current main still documents and implements inbound channel auto-reply runs as per-session serialized work that then enters the process-wide `main` global lane by default, capped by `agents.defaults.maxConcurrent`. Arbitrary lane plumbing exists and the gateway `agent` protocol can accept a `lane`, but I found no config surface for per-channel lane assignment, no `agents.defaults.lanes` schema, and no browser-tool auto-routing to a dedicated lane. The related items do not supersede it: #10334 was addressed by a subagent announce drain fix, #29685 by browser cleanup work, and #16055 is closed stale rather than tracking the remaining per-channel/browser lane routing request.
