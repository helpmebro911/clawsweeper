## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still does not support an object-form `memorySearch.fallback` with separate `remote` or `model` settings, and the fallback path still reuses the primary remote config. The PR also appears to bundle unrelated SSRF/compaction changes, so there is no strong basis for conservative closure.
