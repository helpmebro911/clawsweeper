## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` at `38caa6832d4e1d344736d635b0c5fdad37e4430b` still implements only one primary cron delivery target plus an optional failure destination; the proposed `additionalTargets` fan-out is not present in the types, gateway schema, delivery planner, CLI surface, or docs.
