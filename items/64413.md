## Summary

Current `main` still exposes coarse cron delivery states (`delivered | not-delivered | unknown | not-requested`) and task delivery states (`pending | delivered | session_queued | failed | parent_missing | not_applicable`) without a shared normalized delivery-reason field or protocol/UI surface for codes like `not_configured`, `channel_unavailable`, or `rate_limited`. The issue remains actionable and is not stale.
