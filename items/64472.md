## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still rejects workspace bootstrap symlinks once their canonical target leaves the workspace root, even if the target stays under `~/.openclaw`, and the bootstrap loader converts that failure into a missing-file entry. The issue timeline also already links open follow-up PRs (`#64473`, `#51277`), which is evidence that a fix is still pending rather than shipped.

There is no special-case allowance for `~/.openclaw` siblings such as `~/.openclaw/kb`.
