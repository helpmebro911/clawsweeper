## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement the PR’s claimed hard approval boundary for destructive tools in external-content hook sessions, and the `wrapWebContent` warning change is also not present on `main`. The PR may still be useful, but it is not already implemented and there is not strong evidence to close it for another reason.
