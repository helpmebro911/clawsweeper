## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still has the exact inconsistency the PR fixes: `updateLastRoute` acquires `withSessionStoreLock` but reads via `loadSessionStore(storePath)` without `skipCache: true`, while the other lock-holding writers re-read from disk inside the lock. Nothing in current `main` shows this was implemented elsewhere.

## Evidence
