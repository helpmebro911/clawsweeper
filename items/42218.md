## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` does not implement this PR's core behavior: Feishu interactive-card quote parsing is still limited to top-level `div` and `markdown` elements, and `getMessageFeishu` still fetches messages without the `card_msg_content_type=raw_card_content` parameter needed for richer card payloads.
