## Summary

Keep open. Current main still lacks automatic WhatsApp ack reaction cleanup after a reply: WhatsApp sends the ack reaction and supports manual reaction removal through the reaction tool, but the inbound ack lifecycle never tracks the ack promise or calls the shared post-reply removal helper, and the WhatsApp ackReaction config schema remains strict without a remove/removeAckAfterReply option.
