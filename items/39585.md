## Summary

Keep open. Current main has useful Telegram-native exec approval delivery and a general `/elevated full`/exec-policy workaround, but it does not implement the requested Telegram group trusted-owner approval bypass or policy matrix. The requested owner x group x tool-class behavior remains meaningfully missing.

## Evidence
