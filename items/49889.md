## Summary

Keep open. Current main has internal Telegram lane result kinds and some hook parity for successful preview finalization, but it still does not provide the clear operator-visible delivery-mode observability requested by #49889. In particular, successful `sent` and `preview-finalized` outcomes collapse into generic `message_sent` success signals, `preview-retained` is treated as delivered without a distinct event mode, and hook-canceled delivery is skipped without a corresponding final outcome event.

## Evidence
