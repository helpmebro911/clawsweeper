## Summary

Keep #47852 open. Current main partially covers the use case through `agents.list[].params`, and it is documented for per-agent `temperature` and `maxTokens`; however the broader requested generation surface is not fully implemented. The proposed `generation` config block is not present, and the generic stream-param wrapper only maps a small subset of params, not `topP`/`top_p`, `topK`/`top_k`, `presencePenalty`, or `frequencyPenalty`. The related #59168 is broader model-key/config-profile work and does not supersede this per-agent generation-parameter request.
