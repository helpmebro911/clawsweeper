## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still contains and tests the cron-to-nested lane remap that the report calls out, while the subagent cleanup/runtime-shim concern appears at least partially addressed by a dedicated `subagent-registry.runtime` entry and packaging coverage. That makes this issue mixed rather than resolved, and there is also an open linked PR (#69240) working the cron side.
