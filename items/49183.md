## Summary

Keep open. Current main has useful channel/group `toolsBySender` policy support and owner-only gating, but the RFC's central request remains unimplemented: there is no `toolsByScope` config and no operator-scope input in the effective agent tool-policy resolver. The linked open PRs #53530 and #53496 are relevant incremental slices, but they do not supersede the requested operator-scope-to-tool-policy bridge while still open.
