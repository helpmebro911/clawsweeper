## Summary

Keep #23916 open. Current main partially improves pairing clarity by showing requested vs approved access, roles/scopes, tokens, and IP, and the underlying pairing record now carries client metadata. But the human `openclaw devices list` output still does not show `clientId`, `clientMode`, platform/device family, host/user-agent-style details, a user-editable note/name, or a `--full` view, so the main requested capability remains missing.
