## Summary

Keep open. Current main has generic plugin service lifecycle hooks and newer channel lifecycle helpers, but it does not implement the proposed centralized per-account PluginResourceManager for channel plugins. The motivating Feishu cleanup pattern still exists on main, and related #48183/#48588 remain active rather than superseded by a landed fix.

## Evidence
