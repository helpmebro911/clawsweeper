## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` at `7d9172d5e093435d37ed3e00e476a4eaf84a252b` still defaults `tools.fs.workspaceOnly` to `false`, still builds unrestricted host `read`/`write`/`edit` tools when that flag is unset or `false`, and still has tests asserting those unrestricted behaviors. The PR’s secure-by-default flip and sensitive-path denylist are not implemented on `main`.
