## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still contains the warning the issue reports, still permits embedded/replay `read` tool-call blocks that have generic `arguments`/`input` objects without validating a `path`, and only enforces `path` later at read-tool execution time. I did not find evidence in the checked-out code/history that the underlying embedded caller was identified or fixed, and the warning still lacks the run/session correlation the report asked for.
