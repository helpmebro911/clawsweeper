## Summary

Keep open. Current main still treats Feishu @所有人 (`@_all`) as a bot mention, and the Feishu config schema/docs do not expose `ignoreAtAll` or `ignoreBroadcast`. The linked PR #59422 is relevant evidence of attempted work, but it remains open and is not proof that main has implemented the request.
