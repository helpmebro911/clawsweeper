## Summary

Keep open. Current main has the related #70678 quiet-session watchdog fix, but #70856 still reports broader active WhatsApp listener instability: retryable 408/428 reconnect churn, outbound `No active WhatsApp Web listener` gaps, health-monitor restarts during reconnect, libsignal/Bad MAC errors, and a Linux/Docker group-delivery failure tied to `Failed hydrating participating groups on connect: 1006`. Those remaining symptoms are not fully solved on main.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #70856 open as a real WhatsApp plugin regression. The best maintainer path is to add focused coverage for retryable 408/428 reconnects, ensure the generic health monitor does not stop/start WhatsApp while its own reconnect loop is healthy and bounded, make outbound direct sends wait/queue through short reconnect gaps, and investigate recovery for `groupFetchAllParticipating` 1006 plus libsignal/Bad MAC failures so group events and sends do not silently disappear.
