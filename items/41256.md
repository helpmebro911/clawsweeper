## Summary

Keep open. The linked PRs #41265, #41419, and #41714 describe the same desired fix, but current main at 81a41fe5bee350c6a035bfcc458eb8be380afe2c still constructs Discord upload Blobs without MIME types and still routes WebP images through the JPEG/PNG optimizer path.
