## Summary

Keep open. Current main still treats Discord `requireMention` as a single guild/channel boolean, while `users` is a sender allowlist and `toolsBySender` only scopes tool policy. The requested per-user mention trigger policy is concrete, not implemented, and not superseded by the stale bot comment or an already-posted ClawHub/plugin workaround.
