## Summary

Current `main` already addresses both reports: custom/OpenAI-style provider usage is normalized from `prompt_tokens`/`completion_tokens` into session totals used by `/status`, and isolated cron announce delivery now routes through the outbound adapter instead of relying on subagent-announce fallback.
