## Summary

Current main still sends main-session cron reminder text through the heartbeat model path, so #16142 remains an actionable API-cost/product behavior issue rather than something conservative cleanup should close.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #16142 open until main-session cron reminders can bypass `getReplyFromConfig` for simple relay/formatting cases, or until maintainers explicitly decide model-mediated cron reminder delivery is intentional. A likely implementation belongs around `executeMainSessionCronJob`, `resolveHeartbeatRunPrompt`, or a small deterministic delivery helper, with tests proving simple reminders do not call the model while agentic cron jobs still can.
