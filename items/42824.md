## Summary

Current `main` still has the pre-fix IRC behavior: when `channels.irc.blockStreaming` is unset, IRC falls through to the global `agents.defaults.blockStreamingDefault` instead of requiring an explicit IRC opt-in. The PR remains relevant and additive because it corrects that behavior and adds missing regression coverage.

## Evidence
