## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still reproduces the reported `chat.history` 1000-cap mismatch, so this PR is neither implemented on `main` nor safely closable under the allowed reasons. The duplicate-thread evidence also points to a narrower alternative in PR #66650 that clamps the offending `sessions_history` caller instead of changing the Gateway protocol cap.
