## Summary

Current `main` does not implement the requested Telegram SDK exports, and the repo now documents/tests those Telegram-branded subpaths as intentionally non-public. That is strong evidence against closing this as already fixed, obsolete, or reproducibly resolved; it still needs a maintainer product/compatibility decision.
