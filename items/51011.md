## Summary

Keep PR #51011 open. Current main still does not implement the proposed iOS Privacy & Access settings section, does not add the Contacts/Calendar/Reminders usage-description keys, and does not provide an equivalent in-app permission management surface under another name. The related #50998 remains a narrower Contacts/Calendar first-use permission fix, not a superseding replacement for this Settings UX or Reminders coverage. The PR is active, focused, and has review discussion that belongs in normal iOS feature review rather than cleanup closure.

## Best Possible Solution

Keep #51011 open as an iOS app feature PR.
