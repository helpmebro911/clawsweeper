## Summary

Keep PR #51011 open. Current main still lacks the proposed iOS Privacy & Access settings UI and the required Contacts/Calendar/Reminders usage-description keys, while the existing service paths still require users to grant those permissions elsewhere. Related PR #50998 is narrower service-layer permission work and does not supersede this in-app management UX.

The likely maintainer path is to refine and land a PrivacyAccessSectionView plus matching Info.plist/project.yml usage descriptions, coordinate with #50998 where first-use service prompting overlaps, and verify Contacts, Calendar write/full, and Reminders states on iOS before merge.
