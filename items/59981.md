## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still appears to match the reported bug: Mattermost DMs are documented as non-threaded, but the outbound reply-root helper still falls back to `payload.replyToId` with no direct-message guard, so the issue is not strongly closable as implemented or obsolete.
