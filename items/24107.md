## Summary

Keep open: current main improves cooldown behavior, but the requested generic RPM/RPD-aware provider quotas are not implemented. Provider config/schema still has no rpm/rpd fields, and fallback still relies on recorded profile cooldowns and generic stepped backoff rather than pre-request quota accounting.

## Evidence
