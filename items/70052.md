## Summary

Keep open. Current `main` still uses `@mariozechner/pi-coding-agent`'s plain `estimateTokens(...)` throughout the compaction path, while OpenClaw's CJK-aware weighting remains confined to the pruning/helper path. I did not find a landed fix on `main` or in `v2026.4.23`, so there is not strong evidence to close this conservatively.
